Website Evaluations

Nike

Nike's official website is a premier destination for athletic footwear, apparel, and accessories, showcasing an excellent blend of aesthetics, functionality, and user experience.

  • Contrast: Strong contrast between background and text, as well as vibrant images against a clean backdrop.
  • Repetition: Consistent color scheme, typography, and thematic elements across all pages.
  • Alignment: Well-aligned content with a clear grid structure, making the layout visually appealing.
  • Proximity: Logically grouped related items and information, making navigation intuitive.

Craigslist

Craigslist is a classified advertisements website with a minimalist design that prioritizes functionality over modern aesthetics, often appearing outdated.

  • Contrast: Uses contrast effectively but lacks visual appeal to make important sections stand out.
  • Repetition: Consistent but dated appearance due to basic color scheme and font use.
  • Alignment: Functional and straightforward layout but lacks dynamic engagement.
  • Proximity: Groups related content effectively but can overwhelm with dense packing of links and text.
